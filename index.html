  1. Derive the expression for the Voltage Gain for a Capacitively Coupled Single Tuned Amplifier.

  5. Describe the operation of a Single Tuned Capacitively Coupled Amplifier with a neat circuit diagram.
  6. A Transformer-Coupled Class A amplifier delivers power to an 8 ohm load via a 10:1 transformer. If V_cc = 20V and I_cq = 100mA, find the reflected load and AC output power.
  7. Derive the expression for the Voltage Gain at Resonance for a Capacitively Coupled Single Tuned Amplifier.
  8. A Transformer-Coupled Class A amplifier delivers power to a 4 ohm load via a 5:1 transformer. If V_cc = 10V and I_cq = 50mA, find the reflected load and AC output power.
  9. Explain the operation of a Double Tuned Amplifier. Discuss the effect of the coupling coefficient k on the gain-bandwidth product.
  10. Derive the expression for the Voltage gain of a double tuned amplifier.
  11. Derive the expression for the maximum theoretical efficiency of a Transformer Coupled Class A Amplifier. Show that the maximum efficiency is 50%.
